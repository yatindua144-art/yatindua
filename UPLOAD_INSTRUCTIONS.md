# Replace the portfolio on Vercel

These are the prepared source files for the new Yatin Dua policy and writing portfolio.

1. Open the GitHub repository `yatindua144-art/yatindua`.
2. Choose **Add file**, then **Upload files**.
3. Upload the contents of this folder, preserving the `app` and `public` folders.
4. Commit the upload to the `main` branch.
5. Wait for Vercel to redeploy, then open `https://yatindua.vercel.app`.

Do not delete the temporary review site until the Vercel address shows the new portfolio correctly.
